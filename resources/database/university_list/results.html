I am temporarily unable to take additional nominations, but look for a new survey within the next couple of months. Note that the comments people seem to make most when nominating a homepage include the following characteristics:
  • Attractive, yet limited graphics.
  • Easy to find information. In particular people seem to want to be able to find:
    • Admissions information
    • Alumni information
    • Campus Maps
    • Email directories
    • Information about the surrounding community
    • Research and Departmental Programs
    • US Postal Address
  • Short pages. If a user needs to scroll down the page more than once, you might want to consider breaking the information down into several pages.
